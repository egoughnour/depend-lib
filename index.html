Depend-lib

Why? Because sometimes you want to find some structural information in your source or other non-binary files. In a very general sense.

How it works

Right now I'm contemplating a workflow something like this:

Preprocessing

  1. General preprocessing
  2. Per-file preprocessing
  3. Optional input file list ## Execution
  4. a visitor pattern or perhaps
  5. a method for dividing the source into addressable, referenceable or otherwise meaningfully separate parts

How to use

Pass in some indication of what you want out. Such as the smallest non-trivial eigenvector of your Laplacian matrix. Then you can do some agglomeration over the scalars that make up the vector.
